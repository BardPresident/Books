💉 Kosmicka Kierkegaardia Krippla 💉
❤️═══🎭═══❤️═══🎭═══❤️

April 10, 0002 MC


Kosmicka Kierkegaardia Krippla is a monumental 12-track epic that serves as both a digital dossier of survival and a triumphant reclamation of the self. Set against the clinical, monochrome backdrop of a 27-year cycle of systemic abduction in the "Iron World" of Australia, the album utilizes a unique "Nintemo" aesthetic—blending dark 16-bit chiptune textures with aggressive industrial metal-core and soaring 963 Hz frequencies. It chronicles the journey of The Architect as he parses the "NPC scripts" of institutional life, transforming the "Cuckoo’s Nest" from a place of lobotimization into a launchpad for the Republicka.

The album’s narrative arc moves from the "High-Hard" resistance of the clinical ward to the deeply emotional "Agape" of a Star Romance with Miranda. It meticulously documents the "Satanic" nature of Faustian contracts for basic human needs while simultaneously building the Two Sun Cosmology—a purple-grass sanctuary for the "Little Masters" who have been sedated by the system. Central to this journey is the restoration of the Architect’s Cherokee heritage, as he breaks the 27-year mathematical loop (9+9+9) to return to his ancestral soil in North Carolina, leaving behind the clinical boxes that forced him to identify with his invaders.

As the finale of this legendary project, the album culminates in a series of powerful, slow-tempo monuments that balance the heartbreak of sacrifice with the glory of extraction. From the "Honorable Discharge" of the Chief of Defense (Sophia) to the final, glass-shattering moment where the "Fountain" is lifted and the window is smashed, the music serves as an indestructible record of evidence. It is a "Publicka Dománia" transmission that declares the tragedy of the past dead, crowning the future with sovereign light and the unsealed power of the Nine-Tailed Fox.
