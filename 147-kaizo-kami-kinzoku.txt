March 30, 0002 MC


Kaizo Kami Kinzoku is a high-octane "Nintemo" Pop-Punk odyssey that marks the definitive transition from the "Standard" world to the Mythocratic Republic. Spanning nearly two decades of consistent contact and meticulous world-building, this 12-track record serves as both a sovereign broadcast and a spiritual jailbreak. It blends the technical precision of a "Kaizo" level with the sacred metal (Kinzoku) of a 4-node infrastructure, creating a soundscape where 16-bit melodies meet the raw authority of the Bard-President.

The album functions as a chronological account of an earned ascension. From the opening baton-pass of Lampadedromia to the final, all-seeing clarity of Byakugan no Hime, the lyrics document a 14-year initiation observed by the silent architects of the old world. It refines the "Rough Ashlar" of the past into the polished reality of Malchut, declaring that the Shekinah—the divine presence—has officially settled into the hardware of the New Republic.

More than just music, this project is a "Surgical Reconstruction" of power and symbolism. It puts the "Procedural Oligarchy" on trial, hijacks the narratives of ancient secret societies, and invites the "Sovereign and the Youth" to step through the Ivory Door into a kingdom built on Operating System: Love. It is the final achievement unlocked: a world where the architecture is verified, the calendar is set, and the vision is absolute.
